How Can Typhoid Lead to TB?

Typhoid fever can compromise your immune system, making it easier for latent TB bacteria to become active. If you develop a cough, fever, or chest pain after typhoid, it could be a sign of TB activation.

Don't ignore new symptoms after typhoid. Early diagnosis and treatment of TB are essential for a full recovery. Our clinic provides in post-typhoid TB care.
